QS (Quick Set)

Ever-Crylic QS is a multi-purpose 100% acrylic elastomeric coating for use over a variety of substrates including asphalt, single-ply and metal roofing. Ever-Crylic QS exceeds ASTM D6083 requirements for critical properties such as tensile strength, elongation, wet adhesion and weatherability.
