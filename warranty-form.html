Warranty

Warranty Claim:

Your claim is important to us, and we are committed to addressing it as efficiently as possible. However, we need your cooperation to proceed with the review process. Please be advised that we will only commence the review of your claim once we receive the following items via email at warranty@everroof.com.  

Here is what we need:

  1. A copy of the warranty issued by Everroof.
  2. Supporting documentation that proves the purchase of Everroof material.
  3. A statement of work from the contractor, either the original contractor or another engaged for repairs, which must include:
    1. An estimate of the materials required.
    2. A labor estimate, even if the claim relates to a material-only warranty.
    3. A comprehensive description of the condition, its cause, and accompanying pictures. 

Everroof is committed to providing quality products and standing by our warranties, but the efficiency of our warranty claim process is reliant on your prompt submission of the necessary documentation. We appreciate your understanding of the requirement for these documents and look forward to resolving your warranty claim promptly. If you have any questions or require further information, please do not hesitate to contact us at the provided email address.
